# Purdue Outing Club 2026 Elections

## Election Dates

April 6, 2026 and April 13, 2026

## April 6, Before General Meeting

Admin board appoints event coordinators (POCAR Race Coordinator, POCAR Volunteer Coordinator, POCAR Registration Coordinator, POCtoberfest Coordinators, and Climbing Competition Coordinator) and Secretary of Sports

## April 6, During General Meeting

Club members elect the Club Goober, President/Vice President, Secretary of Outreach, Secretary of Operations, Treasurer, Health & Safety consultant, Fundraising & Sponsorship Coordinator, Alumni Coordinator, Diversity & Community Outreach Consultant, Webmaster, and Data Analyst

## April 13, Before General Meeting

Club members elect the Gear Lord, head sports officers, Social Events Coordinators, Social Media Coordinators, and Graduate & Nontraditional Member Coordinator

## April 13, During General Meeting

Current and future Admin board members appoint the sports officers, Chief Angler, and Gear Gremlins
