**Election Date:** November 17, 2025  

**Positions Open**  
- Head Caving Officer
- Gear Lord
- Fundraising and Sponsorship Coordinator
- Graduate and Nontraditional Member Coordinator
- Canoeing Officer (2 spots)
- Caving Officer (1 spot)
- Climbing Officer (1 spot)
- Backpacking Officer (1 spot)
- Whitewater officer (3 spots)

In order to be eligible to run for a sports officer position, you must be a fully approved trip leader
for that sport. 

Please reach out to the officer who holds the position(s) you are interested in. They can help you to understand what's
involved with that position. You can find their contact info on the [pleadership page](/pleadership).

You can view the specific roles and responsibilities for each position [here](/docs/roles_and_responsibilities.pdf).

**How to Run**  
1. Have someone nominate you for the position(s) you would like to run for. You must be nominated by at least one club
member other than yourself.
2. Accept your nomination via the automated email.
3. Create a slide about yourself and why you should be elected.
4. Upload your slide via the link in the second automated email (AFTER you've accepted your nomination).
5. Present your slide and run for your position at the November 17th meeting.

**How to Nominate Another Club Member**  
If you want to nominate another club member for an open position, fill out the [nomination form](https://forms.cloud.microsoft/r/ViJbJ3ZRUC).
